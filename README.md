# Apple Inc. DCF Valuation Model

A 5-year Discounted Cash Flow model built in Excel to determine 
Apple's intrinsic value and compare it to its current market price.

## What it does
- Projects 5 years of free cash flows using historical growth rates
- Applies WACC as the discount rate to calculate present values
- Calculates terminal value to capture value beyond the 5 year window
- Derives an implied share price and compares to current market price

## Key Finding
Implied share price of $103 versus Apple's current market price of $254, 
suggesting the stock trades at a 59% premium to intrinsic value.

## Tools Used
Microsoft Excel

## How to Open
Download Apple_DCF.xlsx and open in Microsoft Excel or Numbers.
